What You Should Watch On Netflix Quiz

This program will help you deside what to watch on Netflix.
When you enter an answer a question you can either enter the the actual answer or the number associated with it.
Example:

Would you like to watch something on Netflix?
1: Yes
2: No

You could either enter 'Yes' or '1' to go down that chain or 'No' or '2' to go down that chain.
Capitalization doesn't matter, but spelling does.
